DISB
DISB API provide facility to access Drug Information Service Bundle(DISB) to get additional information about Substance, Generic Drug and Medicine.
DISB -
Description - Retrives additional information about given Substance, Clinical Drug (CD) i.e Generic, or Real Clinical Drug (RCD) i.e Medicine from DISB utility.
Parameters -
  • id
